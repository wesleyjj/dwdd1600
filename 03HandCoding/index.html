Home

Welcome to Backpacking U.S.A! Our goal is to make your hiking/backapcking/trail running experience the best. We use your fellow outdoorsmen and outdoorswomen to give you the best places to find water, sleep, hike, and explore.

About this Site

This site is composed of three main sections: trails, map, and community.

Trails
Shows the nearest trails in your location.
Map
Lets you view the trails in your location or in a selected location.
Community
Where you share your knownledge and view other users' posts.

As you use this site, you can contribute to the community and help other avid outdoorsmen and outdoorswomen know what you know.

Ways You Can Contribute

Here are a few ways you can contribute to this community:

  1. Use the site.
  2. Add features you know about to trails.
  3. Share the types of equipment you use for different trails.
  4. Give us feedback how we can improve.

Where Do We Get Our Data?

We use the following resources to get our data:

  • National Parks Service
  • OpenStreetMap
  • mapbox
  • overpass turbo
  • YOU!

The most important part of our data is you! You are the one walking these trails. Your insights and information about them make what we do possible.
